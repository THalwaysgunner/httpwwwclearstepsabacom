Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Therapy

At CLEARSTEPS, we provide structured, purpose-driven ABA therapy that helps children grow with clarity, confidence, and consistency.
Our work is grounded in evidence, guided by compassion, and designed to support families every step of the way.

It’s the first step toward building a clear, individualized plan for your child. Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) direct care uses proven, data-driven strategies to build critical skills and reduce barriers to learning.

  • ABA at CLEARSTEPS focuses on meaningful, sustainable progress skills that translate into everyday life and reduce the stress families often carry alone.
  • An ABA assessment is a comprehensive evaluation that identifies strengths, challenges, and opportunities for growth.

Our Services

Individualized Treatment Plan

Every child receives a personalized plan tailored to their abilities, needs, and goals, designed with clarity and delivered with care.

In-Home Therapy

Sessions take place in the comfort of your home, where children feel safe and routines are natural. Learning happens where life happens.

Behavior Management

We address challenging behaviors through function-based strategies that build emotional regulation and long-term stability. Our focus is always on teaching, not punishing.

Skill Development

Our therapy emphasizes the development of key skills, including communication, social skills, and daily living skills

Parent & Caregiver Training

We empower families with practical tools and step-by-step guidance, helping you support progress far beyond the session.

Community-Based Services

We help children practice and strengthen skills in real-world settings so progress transfers into daily life.

How it works

At CLEARSTEPS, we are committed to guiding you through every step of the enrollment process to ensure a smooth transition into ABA therapy services for your child.

Client Intake
Insurance Verification
We verify your insurance coverage, explaining your benefits and any cost-sharing responsibilities
Documentation Collection
Our team will gather all necessary diagnostic documents, including evaluations, IEP ( if applicable), doctor referrals, and the Client Intake Packet. This process typically takes 1-2 weeks
Initial Authorization Request
We submit a request for assessment authorization to your insurance provider on your behalf

Assessment and Service Setup
Availability Coordination
We work with you to understand your child's needs and service schedule preferences.
Assessment
A Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) will conduct your child's assessment. This includes a discussion with you, direct observation, and the creation of a personalized treatment plan.
Assessment Review
The treatment plan is reviewed and submitted to your insurance for approval of ongoing services.
Staffing
Using our matching process, we locate a skilled Behavior Technician (BT) or Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) in your area to support your child. This step typically takes about 2-3 weeks.

Initial Service Period
Service Coordination
Your BCBA and BT will coordinate schedules to begin therapy sessions.
Initial Check-Ins
We ensure the BT is pairing well with your child and that therapy sessions are proceeding smoothly.
Ongoing Communication
During the first 4 weeks of services, we maintain consistent check-ins to monitor progress and address any concerns.

Ongoing ABA Services
Case Management
Once services are established, a Case Manager will oversee your child's care.
Service Consistency
We monitor therapy sessions for consistency and progress,addressing cancellations and making necessary adjustments.
Reassessment & Reauthorization
Periodic reassessments are conducted ( typically every 6 months), and we manage the reauthorization process to ensure uninterrupted care.
Insurance Support
If your insurance changes, we'll help you navigate the process to maintain services.
